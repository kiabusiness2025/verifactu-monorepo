## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please report suspected vulnerabilities privately to **soporte@verifactu.business**.

- Include a clear description, impact, and steps to reproduce.
- Do not open public issues for security findings.
- We will acknowledge within 5 business days and share a remediation timeline after assessment.
